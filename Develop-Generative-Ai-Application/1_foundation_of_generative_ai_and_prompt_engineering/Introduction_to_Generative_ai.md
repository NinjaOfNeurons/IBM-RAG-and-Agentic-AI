## 1. Introduction to Artificial Intelligence (AI)

**Artificial Intelligence (AI)** is the simulation of human intelligence by machines.
AI systems are designed to perform tasks such as learning, reasoning, problem-solving, and decision-making.

### Types of AI

AI can be broadly categorized into:

1. **Discriminative AI**

   * Classifies or predicts outcomes based on input data.
   * Example: Spam detection, image classification.

2. **Generative AI**

   * Creates new content based on learned patterns from training data.
   * Example: Text generation, image creation.

## 4. How Generative AI Develops Creativity

Generative AI uses advanced model architectures:

### 1. Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs)

* Two neural networks compete to improve output quality.

### 2. Variational Autoencoders (VAEs)

* Learn compressed representations of data and generate variations.

### 3. Transformers

* Use attention mechanisms.
* Power modern Large Language Models (LLMs).

### 4. Diffusion Models

* Generate data by gradually removing noise.
* Commonly used in image generation.
