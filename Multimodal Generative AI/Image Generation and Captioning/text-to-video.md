# OpenAI Sora – Text-to-Video Notes

## 1️ Overview of Sora
- Multimodal, diffusion-based transformer by OpenAI
- Generates **high-quality videos** from **text or image prompts**
- Applications:
  - Creative storytelling (VR, AR, games)
  - Content creation with custom characters/scenes
  - Video editing (upscaling, interpolation, gap-filling)
  - Synthetic data generation (tracking, segmentation, action recognition)
- Similar tools:
  - **RunwayML Gen-3 Alpha** – cinematic output, motion control
  - **Pika Labs** – stylized animated clips for storyboarding
  - **Google Lumiere** – smooth, coherent video motion

## 2️ Workflow for Video Generation
1. **Write a descriptive text prompt**
2. **Send prompt to Sora**
3. **Receive generated video clip**

### Prompt Crafting – 3 Key Elements
- **Scene context:** Environment, location, weather
- **Visual details:** Lighting, color, camera angles
- **Motion:** Movement style, camera pans, slow-motion
- Cinematic language improves results

## 3️ Using the Sora Interface
- Open: [sora.openai.com](https://sora.openai.com)
- Log in / Sign up with OpenAI account
- Dashboard navigation:
  - **Explore:** Browse videos for inspiration
  - **Composer:** Enter your prompt and create videos
- Settings before generating:
  - Type: Video
  - Aspect ratio, resolution, duration
  - Number of variations, style presets

### Example Prompt
> "A black cat walks confidently across a sunlit rooftop at golden hour in a bustling city. Warm, low-angle sunlight casts long shadows as a drone camera slowly circles the cat, revealing the skyline. Rich, cinematic color tones with a hint of slow motion highlight the cat's graceful movements."

- Hit **Enter** or **Create Video**
- Videos appear in **My Videos** after processing (30 sec – few min)
- Hover for preview, click to view in lightbox, navigate variations

## 4️ Editing & Refining
- **Editing toolbar options**:
  - **Remix:** Change parts of video via natural language (e.g., black cat → fluffy orange cat)
  - **Blend:** Combine with another video
  - **Loop:** Make seamless repeating sections
  - **Recut / Storyboard:** Trim or extend video sections
- Add videos to **Favorites**, Like, Share, Download

## 5️ Summary
- Sora = **multimodal diffusion-based model**
- Generates videos from **text or image inputs**
- Structured prompts with scene, visuals, and motion yield **better results**
- Editing tools allow **refinements via natural language**
